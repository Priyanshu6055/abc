FAQ's – Ideate Cohort 2025- SHEvolution

  • expand_more1. What is the objective of the Ideate Cohort 2025 program?

    The Ideate Cohort 2025 is a 10-week capacity-building and pre-incubation program designed for idea-stage startups. Its aim is to impart knowledge about essential concepts and instruments of entrepreneurship to transform ideas into Minimum Viable Products (MVPs).

  • expand_more2. What is the duration of the program?

    The program spans 10 weeks, focusing on capacity building and pre-incubation for idea-stage startups.

  • expand_more3. What is the cost to participate in the program?

    There’s no cost involved for program. Yet, if you want some specific services, like company registration, IPR registration etc, that cost would be as per actuals and would be bear by the startup/participant.

  • expand_more4. Can I apply if my startup is not yet incorporated?

    Yes, startup incorporation is not mandatory at this stage. You may incorporate during the program or at a later stage.

  • expand_more5. What is the structure of the program?

    The program sessions will be conducted online, most likely scheduled on Saturdays. A detailed training schedule, including exact dates, will be shared with selected participants closer to the starting date.

  • expand_more6. What support and resources are provided during the program?

    Participants receive mentorship, guidance, and access to resources such as workshops, networking opportunities, investor connect and advisory services to help develop their ideas into viable businesses.

  • expand_more 7. Can I apply as a solo founder?

    Yes, solo women founders can apply. You can choose to continue the journey on your own or add team members later.

  • expand_more8. Is this a certified program?

    Yes, participants will receive certificates at the end of the pre-incubation period. Additionally, ideas developed into MVPs will have the chance to pursue further incubation facilities.
