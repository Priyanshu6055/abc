IDEATE COHORT 2025- SHEvolution

10 week capacity building and pre-incubation program This program aims at a transformative program designed to empower women innovators and entrepreneurs by helping them convert ideas into impactful ventures. Under this, the women led startups in their ideation stage with no MVP and customer segment but a proof of concept would be guided to form a team, build a sustainable business model and learn nuances of market and entrepreneurship. Backed by the AIC-RNTU Foundation and supported by NITI Aayog, this initiative nurtures women- led startups in tech-focused sectors through hands-on mentoring, training, and access to essential resources.
